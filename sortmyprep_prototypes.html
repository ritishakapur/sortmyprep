SortMyPrep.
Clarity in the Chaos.

The IB journey is fragmented across countless drives, scattered notes, and uncalibrated expectations. We built a high-performance ecosystem to centralize your workflow.

Why this exists

Students waste hundreds of hours managing the meta-work of studying rather than actually learning. SortMyPrep implements a production-ready architecture to unify data-driven gap analysis, community-curated resources, and intelligent study streaks. By replacing friction with liquid-smooth UI and hardware-accelerated workflows, we keep you focused on what matters: the score.
